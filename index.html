BPrep

Conveniently minimize interruptions to business operations before a weather emergency hits your business. B.Prep helps you to prioritize, prepare and manage an emergency plan for your business.

Experience B.Prep

Behind the App

Inspiration

Small business owners often don’t have the resources to setup a complex emergency messaging system. BPrep is focused on easing communication between owners and employees during a weather emergency.

Motivation

Prior to BPrep, small business owners had limited options when it came to preparing for floods. By focusing on communication, our clients can turn their attention to getting up and running after a weather emergency as soon as possible.

Execution

By using multiple weather APIs in conjunction with the Sea Level Rise app, BPrep is able to get the latest weather and flood alerts within seconds. This allows us to provide clients with the latest information to give them enough time to prepare.

Features

Alerts Tailored to your Business

Don't waste another valuable second writing messages to employees and managers. Our set templates allow you to send with one button or customize them as you need them.

Seamless Integration

Uploading contacts and setting up your business is a breeze with our quick and easy setup.

Connecting with Your Team: One Button Away

It's as easy as one, two, three: an alert will appear in your screen prompting you to active your emergency storm plan when a storm is imminent. All with one click.

Case Uses

Small Business Owner

The typical small business owner often does not have the resources to invest in a regular emergency messaging system. However, when a flood happens, it’s difficult to reach all employees, making the process of communication painful and inefficient. By creating a system that centralizes all communications in one easy, accessible and affordable app, we are allowing small business owners to minimize inefficiencies, save time and resources.

Store Manager

When it comes to a slightly larger operation, managers must make decisions instead of the owner while being cost-effective. BPress cuts through the red tape and allows managers to set up a system within minutes with the tools they are already using to manage their employees.

Employee

Employee of Small Business: When a flood comes, employees have different concerns, from picking up children from school to figuring out how to get home in the middle of a flood that has covered the streets. By providing with a system that allows quick employee check-ins, employees can keep up to date with their managers and get back to work safely and efficiently.
