BPrep prevents and minimizes interruptions to small business operations caused by weather emergencies.

By centralizing communications in one easy to use app, BPrep streamlines the process of contacting staff members during an emergency situation, allowing business owners to save time and resources.

Behind the App

Inspiration

Small business owners don’t often have the resources to setup a complex emergency messaging system. BPrep is focused on facilitating communication between owners and employees before and during a weather emergency, automating valuable processes that save business owners time and money.

Motivation

Prior to BPrep, small business owners had limited options when it came to preparing for floods and organizing their workplace for a weather emergency. By focusing on communication, our clients can better prepare for a weather emergency, stay up to date with their employees and minimize losses resulting from a flood.

Execution

By using multiple weather APIs in conjunction with the Sea Level Rise app, BPrep is able to get the latest weather and flood alerts within seconds. This allows us to provide clients with the latest information to give them enough time to prepare and get organized.

Features

Alerts Tailored to your Business

Don't waste another valuable second writing messages to employees and managers. Our set templates allow you to send messages with one button and customize them as you need.

Seamless Integration

Uploading contacts and setting up your business emergency plan is a breeze with our quick and easy setup. Simply upload a Google Sheet with all your contact information or follow our manual add process. Within minutes, all your staff's emergency contact information is stored in a central and secure database.

Connecting with Your Team: One Button Away

It's as easy as one, two, three! An alert will appear in your screen prompting you to activate your emergency storm plan when a storm is imminent. All with one click.

A Few Use Cases

Small Business Owner

The typical small business owner often does not have the resources to invest in a regular emergency messaging system. However, when a flood happens, it’s difficult to reach all employees, making the process of communication painful and inefficient. By creating a system that centralizes all communications in one easy, accessible and affordable app, we are allowing small business owners to minimize inefficiencies, save time and resources.

Store Manager

When it comes to a slightly larger operation, managers must make decisions instead of the owner while being cost-effective. BPress cuts through the red tape and allows managers to set up a system within minutes with the tools they are already using to manage their employees.

Employee

Employee of Small Business: When a flood comes, employees have different concerns, from picking up children from school to figuring out how to get home in the middle of a flood that has covered the streets. By providing with a system that allows quick employee check-ins, employees can keep up to date with their managers and get back to work safely and efficiently.
