B.Prep

Conveniently minimize interruptions to business operations before a weather emergency hits your business. B.Prep helps you to prioritize, prepare and manage an emergency plan for your business.

Experience B.Prep

Inspiration

Our team worked hard to come up with an app that would ease the stress of managing a small business during an impeding weather emergency. Our goal: come up with a solution that addressed the issues of a largely untapped market.

Motivation

Our extensive research showed us that to be able to help them, we needed to tackle one very big issue: communication. We wanted to make that process easier with all the technologies available at our hands.
